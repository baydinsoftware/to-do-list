To-do how-to:

Accounts:

  • You can create an account, by going to the registration page
  • Once your account has been created, you will be logged in and a to-do list will be created with your name and account information. You do not need to create a list.
  • If you forget your password, press the "Recover your password" link on the login page, or click here
    • Once you have provided a valid email address, a link with instructions to reset your password will be sent to you.

Lists:

  • A to-do list with your name and account information will be created upon registration. The main page contains the to-do list for all the users.
  • Lists have different functionalities, depending on whether or not you are the owner of the list.
  • If you are the owner of the list you are viewing:
    • Click and drag the list items to change the priority of the task.
    • To mark a task as completed, check the check box next to the item. Uncheck it to mark as incomplete.
    • Double click a list item to view more information on the task.
  • If you are not the owner of the list you are viewing:
    • Single click a list item to view more information on the task.
  • Filter lists (owner and non-owner) by using the drop-down menu above the list.
  • You can assign tasks to users by pressing the link that says "Add an item to X's to-do list."
    • All items except the due date are required.
